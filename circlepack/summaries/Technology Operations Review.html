Technology Operations Review


  • This applies to all major products, specifically outlined in the TOR schedule 

  • Heads of Engineering lead an operational review of their product each week

  • Each Head of Engineering will present roughly four times a year on a rotational basis.



  • A meeting lead by Heads of Engineering to present, to tech leadership, the efficiency, effectiveness and economics of their products. e.g. profitability, code and squad health and security posture.

  • It aims to improve our ability to make deliberate, meaningful and regular improvements in a sustainable and organised manner.


If your product / system is not covered in a TOR, this reduces visibility of its operational health, which may lead to:

  • a lack of investment and opportunity for uplift

  • a lack of awareness of current state and challenges across the wider technology leadership group



Engineering Enablement


  • 1 hour weekly to attend the operational review for Heads of Engineering and potentially Engineering Team Leads 

  • Approximately 1 day for Heads of Engineering and relevant Engineering Team leads to prepare for their review. They will present roughly 4 times a year.
