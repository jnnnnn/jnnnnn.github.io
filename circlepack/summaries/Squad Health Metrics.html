Squad Health Metrics


  • This applies to you if you’re a member of a squad at Iress which develops or maintains software.

  • The Engineering Team Lead is responsible for making sure an initial assessment is performed.

  • The Engineering Team Lead is responsible for making sure the assessment is reviewed and updated by the squad at least once every three months.


Speed, scale and quality are the three pillars of the Iress Technology strategy, advocated by Andrew Todd, our Chief Technology Officer. Our Health Metrics initiative aims to identify areas where there is an opportunity to lift engineering capability, leading to improved speed, scale and quality.


  • Your squad’s ability to operate as a Product Engineering team will diminish because you don’t know which capabilities to strengthen.

  • It will be challenging to work with other squads because your way of working is significantly different.

  • Squad members will leave the squad.

  • Squad members will feel demotivated.


Delivery Coach


  • Most squads organise a half-day workshop to explore the Squad Health Metric questions, debate the assessment for each metric and create an initial assessment file.

  • Most squads organise a two-hour workshop every three months to review the Squad Health Metric questions and reevaluate the assessments for the squad.

  • Most squads use their Retrospective meetings to brainstorm and agree experiments to improve their health metrics. These sessions should take no more than three hours every month.
