Cloudability 


Visualise Cloud Costs across your AWS environment:

  • Unify IaaS and PaaS spend into one view to drive FinOps best practices and inform decision making across Product and Technology teams 

  • Leverage full portfolio reporting, including cost and utilisation data, to tailor segmented dashboards for stakeholders and clearly articulate the value of your cloud strategy

  • Optimise costs across cloud ecosystem to reinvest in innovation and growth strategies 




Manage and Optimise Cloud Costs:

Ingest, normalise and structure cloud billing and usage data across your public cloud ecosystem to actively manage spend, drive accountability, and continuously improve the unit economics of cloud. 



Global Cloud market has predicted 30% is unnecessary Cloud over spend, so by not using this tool for FinOps, Iress will not be able to address this key problem space to achieve potential cost savings and reinvest in innovation and new projects.
